	• Salary	Grade
	• Salary >=50000	A
	• Salary >= 25000 < 50000	B
	• Salary>=10000 < 25000	C
	• OTHERS	D

  5  WHEN STAFF_SAL  >10000 AND  STAFF_SAL<25000 THEN 'C'
